🌙 After-Hours Available

After-Hours Virtual Consultations

Need a doctor after hours? Get professional medical advice from the comfort of your home — no need to wait until morning.

How It Works

1

Contact Us

WhatsApp or call us between 17:00 and 22:00 to request a virtual consultation.

2

Share Your Medical Aid Details

Provide your medical aid scheme name, membership number, and a brief description of your symptoms.

3

Consult with the Doctor

A doctor will call you back via WhatsApp video call or phone call for your consultation.

4

Get Your Prescription

If medication is needed, your prescription will be sent electronically to your preferred pharmacy.
