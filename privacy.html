Privacy Policy

CareCross Medical Center – Dr. V. Prefa & Partners

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

CareCross Medical Center ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our patients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Physical address
  • Medical history and health information (for clinical purposes only)
  • Medical aid/insurance details
  • Identity or passport number (when required for medical records)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing medical consultations and healthcare services
  • Scheduling and managing appointments
  • Processing medical aid claims and billing
  • Communicating with you about your health and appointments
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Responding to enquiries submitted through our website or lead forms

4. Protection of Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Patient medical records are stored securely and access is limited to authorised medical personnel only.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or regulation
  • With your medical aid provider for claims processing
  • With specialist referral practitioners (with your consent)
  • With laboratories for diagnostic testing purposes

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator

7. Cookies and Website Data

Our website may use basic analytics to improve user experience. We do not use cookies to track personal information or share browsing data with third parties.

8. Lead Form Data

If you submit your details through our Google Ads lead form, your information (name, phone number, email) will be used solely to contact you regarding your healthcare enquiry. This data is handled securely and not shared with unrelated third parties.

9. Retention of Information

We retain patient records for the period required by the Health Professions Act and other applicable legislation. Non-medical enquiry data is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your request.
