About Us

Trusted healthcare in the heart of Piet Retief

Caring for Our Community

CareCross Medical Center is a trusted general practice conveniently located in the Kempville Shopping Center, Piet Retief. We are committed to providing affordable, accessible, and high-quality medical care to the Piet Retief community and surrounding areas.

Led by Dr. Victor Prefa and partners, our team brings extensive clinical experience across multiple countries and a genuine passion for patient care. Whether you need a routine check-up, chronic disease management, or minor surgical procedures, we are here for you.

Our practice offers a full range of general medical services including GP consultations, chronic care management, minor procedures, occupational health assessments, family healthcare, and wellness services — all under one roof.
